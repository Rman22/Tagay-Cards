ABOUT

Tagay card is a straightforward game with three distinct categories. The (Truth or Dare) comes first, followed by (Name a thing, a place, a name, an animal), and finally the Shat or Pass.

Every category has its own set of regulations, which may be found in the top-right corner. If you wish to, you can also establish your own rules.
